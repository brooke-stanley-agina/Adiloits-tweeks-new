Health Industry

Introduction

We couldn't begin with any other industry other than health as it's where the COVID-19 Crisis hinges on.

Rocket Health is a product of the Medical Concierge Group,it offers services like telemedic,pharmacy,laboratory and clinical services. Rockethealth model leverages digital platforms like web and social media platforms as their channels and they have been able to build a community and great customer experience around it.

Mass early restock: Products like Antibacterial disinfectant,Sanitizers, Alcoholic pad,Gloves and face masks. As a quick tweak in their model, the company had to introduce or restock such products at the beginning of the crisis.

Market responsive: Rockhealth didn't just have offerings that are on demand but they have built a great customer experience in ordering and delivery. To maximize the crisis and remain relevant in the market, they altered the order on the UI such that the COVID-19 handy health products are the first you see while navigating on their e-pharmacy store.

Note
Businesses that had already invested in digital infrastructure channels of reaching their customers have more opportunity over their competitors.

Business model hacks for health.

1. Crowdsource and Health information dissemination

The crisis has brought a health panic now more than ever causing patients to seek health information and support urgently.Companies in the health sector have an opportunity to create channels where they can have patients to ask and share information during the crisis.

Resources and activities
A seamless portal to ask,share and receive information.
Massive content creation
24hr online support
Delivery mechanisms
Cautious and secure employee engagement program.
Prebooking

Business opportunities
Community building and organic customer growth.
Customers knowledge and information becomes part of creating solutions for them.
Big data selling through reports
Service scheduling,new product or service offering based on data.

Business model trends
Crowdsourcing
Big data

Business model Characteristics
Online community
Information dissemination

2. Personal health records as a value proposition

The crisis presents an opportunity for medical institutions to create patient databases which have past health records and patients status of the COVID-19 virus. As travel bans loosen there is going to be a high demand for personal health records locally and globally.

What to validate?
Regulations from ministry of health
The possibilities of setting up a quick infrastructure to capture past and current patients records.
Pricing for patients to access the data.

3. Depression and anxiety treatment as a value proposition

People around the world are facing increasing mental health issues during the COVID-19 pandemic.They are at risk of abuse,Loss of businesses, employees facing job uncertainty and children are among those most at risk. This presents an opportunity for businesses in mental health wellbeing to create offerings that can address the problem in homes and at work places.

What to validate?
Talent acquisition for specialists in areas of mental health?
Infrastructure to set up and operate remote therapy?
Demand validation beginning with existing customers?
