Entertainment industry

Introduction

The ban of gatherings is affecting and crushing the entertainment industry so hard from artists,theaters,sports among others.

Businesses in this industry will have to rethink ways they will operate in the next 12-18 months with limited gathering new regulations.

Business model hacks for Supply chain.

1. Virtual/digital performances as a value proposition

As boredom and frustration consume people during the lockdown there is a rising demand for virtual entertainment.Companies that have been in physical entertainment or gathering have to start identifying which of their services can go online and which ones will stay offline.

  • Pre-recording as an activity will be key in executing such an offering.
  • Subscription platform as a resource and revenue.
  • Virtual reality technology will be a trend to consider.
  • Codifying the business model will be required on such offerings.

What to validate ?
Identify the immediate demands that can be offered online
Infrastructure to take the services online.
How to curry the physical experience online.
How to create a community of online consumers?
Test pricing and tactics to lock in customers online and offline
