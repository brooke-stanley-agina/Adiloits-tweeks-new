A collaborative approach of creating business solutions in extreme times of uncertainty.

Adiloits Hacks & tweaks is an open innovation platform created to solve extremely complex problems that affect businesses at massive scale in times of uncertainty. Hacks & tweaks provide value propositions and businesses models for businesses to survive in that complexity.

During the COVID-19 pandemic outbreak, Adiloits had a question of How might we support businesses to thrive during and after the crisis? The answer was Adiloits Hacks & Tweaks.
