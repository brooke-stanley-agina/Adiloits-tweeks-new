Introduction

The transport industry stands as the second crucial industry amidst this crisis,with public means of transport banned there are a few private companies like taxify,safeboda among others that are authorized to conduct business specifically for deliveries. (As of Uganda's COVID 19 restrictions and rules).

Safeboda is a motorcycle transport facilitator hinged on safety,trust and convenience.Leveraging on their existing infrastructure and using a proactive intelligence approach they added other services like Cashless transactions,package delivery, Airtime purchase and food delivery.

New offering : As everyone was panicking,Safeboda was restructuring to be relevant during the crisis.They added new features like shopping which is on a high market demand.

Market response :Due to the increased market demand of delivery and merchandise shopping vs the decline in demand for passenger services , Safeboda altered the order on the UI and brought food,shop and package delivery as the first priorities..

Business model hacks for health.

1. Bike sharing

With the ban on private cars,motorcycle passenger travels and public transport there is an opportunity for business in bike sharing businesses.
Business owners with a stock(inventory) of bikes and platform developers could collaborate to have a platform where one can search and hire a nearby bike to take themselves for shopping or any urgent needs.
One can benchmark on available bike sharing business models like Anywheel,Mobike,SG bike,Ofo among others.

NOTE
The ban of public transport and other transport means isn't permanent,soon the usual transport means will resume, but leveraging on the effect of the crisis to launch Asap is a smart move. One has to consider exploring more pains around the offering and providing long term value propositions beyond the crisis.
