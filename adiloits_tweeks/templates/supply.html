Introduction

The supply supply chain and distribution has been crowded of a lot of players lately especially deliveries of goods or products.

Jumia has tweaked their business model by adding local market vendors on the platform to enable users to order for food items and added a variety of more products. Over 20 startups have popped up to offer ordering of food stuff and deliveries during this crisis.

Business model hacks for Supply chain.

1. Open business geolocation

The crisis presents an opportunity to leverage directory or business listings data,social media businesses and offer an open portal where businesses that are open/ in operation during the crisis can register..

  • With the creation of a digital platform, people in different locations can search,identify and locate businesses available at specific times conveniently.They can order or preorder products & services close to their current location and cut the risks and costs of delivery.
  • Post covid opportunity is the platform will collect data and give updates on businesses that have recovered and not.
  • Recovered and not businesses can be listed on the platform with their needs and their potential in the post Covid wave inorder to attract investors

Benefits
Access of open businesses within your location without risk of movement.
Improvement on declining demands for businesses.
Platform that identifies businesses that survived the crisis and investment opportunities

2. Delivery as service with hygiene at core.

The crisis presents an opportunity to leverage directory or business listings data,social media businesses and offer an open portal where businesses that are open/ in operation during the crisis can register..Because of the pandemic, Most businesses in retail and food have adopted the delivery service of goods to customers,they have also created digital channels to inform customers to pre-order or order for goods.

With the terrifying nature of Covid-19, consumers and organizations are becoming much more careful about the people and products they interact with.Both people and organizations will expect formal/professional proof of hygiene and current health status in;

  • Production and packaging goods or products and ensuring they are delivered under the same conditions.
  • The health status of delivery personnels.

Because of the two factors, its presents an opportunity to;
Packaging and business operations that ensure proper hygiene and non contamination from the virus.
A platform that has capability of health real time monitoring of delivery personnel and means that real time information has to be transferred to the customer to guarantee safety.

What to validate ?
Infrastructure for hygiene in packing and delivery?
